About

A self-taught builder from Nepal, shipping since the lockdown days.

I’m Zenith Kandel, a Grade 12 student at Kathmandu Model Secondary School, Bagbazar. I’ve been obsessed with computers and code since Grade 6 — right when the pandemic pushed everything online, I taught myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript from YouTube tutorials and forum threads, and I haven’t looked back since.

Over the years I’ve picked up PHP and MySQL on the backend, and I’m currently getting comfortable with the MERN stack. I love hackathons — the chaos, the deadline, the demo — and I have a strong history of winning exhibitions. I’m also deeply curious about cybersecurity and bug hunting, and I’ve had the privilege of reporting critical bugs in products like Kantipur Television and Monkeytype.

My work tends to live at the intersection of useful software and real-world problems — public transit, agriculture, education, emergency comms. I’m always learning, always shipping, and always up for the next challenge.

Selected Work

Hackathon projects — built under pressure, demoed to judges, occasionally sleep-deprived.

  1. 01

    Sastomahango

    A crowd-sourced price index that helps Nepalis avoid getting scammed and overpaying for everyday goods. Real prices, reported by real people, in real time.

    • Type
    • Crowdsource · Web
    • Focus
    • Consumer protection
  2. 02

    Lifeline

    An off-grid, RF-based emergency signal system designed for emergencies and remote Nepali communication when cellular and internet infrastructure fails.

    • Type
    • Hardware · RF
    • Focus
    • Emergency comms
  3. 03

    Sawari

    A complete public transportation helper for Kathmandu. Enter a start point and a destination and Sawari plans the entire walkthrough via public buses — fare, road condition, ETA, and the lot.

    • Type
    • Web · Routing
    • Focus
    • Urban mobility
  4. 04

    AgroPan

    A soil monitoring and recommendation system that reads the land through a stack of sensors and tells farmers what their soil actually needs — water, nutrients, attention.

    • Type
    • IoT · Agritech
    • Focus
    • Precision agriculture
  5. 05

    Edu Track Pro

    A smart attendance system for schools in Nepal built around RFID student ID cards. Tap in, tap out, and the rolls are done — accurately, instantly, and auditably.

    • Type
    • Hardware · EdTech
    • Focus
    • School operations
