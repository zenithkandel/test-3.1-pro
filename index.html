01 — THE ORIGIN

It started in
lockdown.

When COVID-19 shut down the world in 2020, a curious Grade 6 student opened a laptop and started learning. YouTube tutorials became the classroom. HTML, CSS, and JavaScript became the language.

What began as a lockdown hobby quickly turned into an obsession. I moved from building simple web pages to engineering full-stack applications — PHP backends, MySQL databases, and later exploring the MERN stack.

But I don't just build things. I break them too. My interest in cybersecurity led me to discover critical vulnerabilities in production systems — including Kantipur Television and the popular typing tool Monkeytype.
